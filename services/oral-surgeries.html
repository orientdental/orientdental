Missing a Tooth is not the end, it's the Start of a New Smile!

Dental implants are the ideal solution for replacing missing teeth — providing long-lasting, natural results while protecting your oral health.

Why Choose Dental Implants?

  • Permanent and long-lasting solution
  • Helps preserve jawbone structure
  • Improved chewing function and aesthetics
  • No damage to neighboring teeth (unlike traditional bridges)

How the Implant Procedure Works

  1. Initial consultation: Detailed assessment by doctors with 30+ years of experience
  2. Implant placement: Precise, painless, and with maximum comfort
  3. Healing & osseointegration: 3–6 months for the implant to fuse with bone
  4. Final restoration: Abutment and permanent crown placement

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there an age limit?

No — even patients over 70 can receive implants if their bone structure is healthy.

Is the procedure painful?

No! With modern local anesthesia and our expertise, you will not feel pain during placement.

How long does it take?

The placement of a single implant can be done in under 20 minutes. Healing takes 3–6 months.

Are there contraindications?

Patients with uncontrolled diabetes or severe bone issues should undergo further evaluation first.

How long does an implant last?

With proper care, a dental implant can last 20 years or more.
