Orthodontic Treatment Steps

Initial Consultation: A thorough examination including photos, X-rays, and discussion of your smile goals.
1
Digital Impressions / X-rays: Advanced imaging creates a precise map of your teeth for treatment planning.
2
Customized Treatment Plan: We tailor your orthodontic journey—choosing between braces or aligners.
3
Application of Braces / Clear Aligners: Your appliance is placed or delivered, and your journey begins.
4
Regular Adjustments & Follow-ups: Routine visits ensure everything is progressing correctly.
5
Final Results and Retainers: Once aligned, retainers maintain your new smile.
6

Braces vs. Clear Aligners

  • Braces: Ideal for complex corrections. Available in metal or ceramic. Fixed and always working.
  • Clear Aligners: Nearly invisible, removable, and comfortable. Best for aesthetic, flexible treatment.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does treatment take?

On average, 12 to 24 months, depending on the complexity and patient compliance.

Are clear aligners as effective as braces?

Yes, for mild to moderate cases. Severe issues may still require braces.

Is orthodontic treatment painful?

Mild discomfort is common after adjustments, but it subsides quickly.

Can I eat normally during treatment?

With braces, avoid hard or sticky foods. Aligners are removed while eating.
