Dental Implants

Losing a tooth is not the end – it's the beginning of a new smile!

1. Why are implants the ideal solution?

• Implants offer permanent and long-lasting solutions
• Implants help preserve jawbone structure
• Improved chewing function and aesthetics
• No damage to neighboring teeth like with traditional bridges

2. How the implant procedure is performed?

• Initial consultation and thorough evaluation by doctors with over 30 years of experience
• Precise, painless implant placement with maximum care for your comfort
• Healing and osseointegration phase (3–6 months after placement)
• Abutment placement and final crown installation

3. Why should you trust us with your smile?

• 30+ years of experience in implantology
• Certified Italian implants
• Competitive pricing and complete transparency from the initial estimate
• Modern technology & collaboration with leading labs in Tirana

FAQ

Is there an age limit?

In most cases, no. Even patients over 70 can get implants if they have healthy bone structure.


Is the procedure painful?

No! With modern local anesthesia and our experience, you won’t feel pain during the implant placement.


How long does the procedure take?

Placing a single implant can take less than 20 minutes. Healing typically takes 3–6 months.


Are there any contraindications?

Patients with uncontrolled diabetes or severe bone issues should consult for further evaluation.


How long does an implant last?

With proper care, a dental implant can last 20 years or more.
